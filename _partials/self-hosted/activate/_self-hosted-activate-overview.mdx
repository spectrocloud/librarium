Below is an overview of the activation process.

    ![Diagram of the self-hosted system activation process](/self-hosted-setup_supported-environments_activate_system-activation-diagram_4.8.webp)

1. The system admin installs {props.version} or upgrades it to version 4.6.32 or later.

2. {props.version} enters trial mode. During this time, you have 30 days to take advantage of all of {props.version}'s features. After
   30 days, the trial expires, and {props.version} functionality is restricted. Any clusters that you have deployed remain
   functional, but you cannot perform
   [day-2 operations](/clusters/cluster-management) or deploy
   additional clusters.

3. Before or after your trial expires, contact a Spectro Cloud customer support representative. You must specify whether
   you are activating Palette or VerteX and also provide a short description of your instance, along with your
   installation's product ID.

4. Spectro Cloud provides the activation key.

5. The system admin enters the activation key and activates {props.version}, allowing you to resume day-2 operations and deploy
   additional clusters.
