Beginning with version 4.6.32, once you install {props.version} or upgrade it to version 4.6.32 or later, you have 30 days to
activate it. During this time, you have unrestricted access to all of {props.version}'s features. After 30 days, you can
continue to use {props.version}, and existing clusters will continue to run, but you cannot perform the following operations
until {props.version} is activated:

- Create new clusters.

- Modify the configuration of active clusters. This includes modifying
  [cluster profile variables](/profiles/cluster-profiles/create-cluster-profiles/define-profile-variables);
  changing [cluster profile versions](/clusters/cluster-management/cluster-updates#enablement);
  editing, deleting, or replacing profile layers; and editing YAML files.

- Update [node configurations](/clusters/cluster-management/node-pool), such as the node pool size.

Each installation of {props.version} has a unique product ID and corresponding activation key. Activation keys are single-use
and valid for the entirety of the {props.version} installation, including all subsequent version upgrades. Once {props.version} is
activated, it does not need to be reactivated unless you need to reinstall {props.version}, at which time a new product ID will
be assigned, and a new activation key will be needed. Activation keys are no additional cost and are included with your
purchase of {props.version}. The activation process is the same for connected and airgapped installations, regardless of whether
{props.version} is installed via the [Palette CLI](/automation/palette-cli),
<PaletteVertexUrlMapper edition={props.edition} text="Helm chart" url="/supported-environments/kubernetes/install/install" />, or
<PaletteVertexUrlMapper edition={props.edition} text="Management Appliance" url="/supported-environments/management-appliance" /> ISO.

If you are in trial mode or your trial has expired, {props.version} displays the appropriate banner on the **Summary** screen of
your system console, as well as at **Administration > Activation**. Trial mode and expired statuses are also displayed in the {props.version} UI at the bottom of the left main menu.
