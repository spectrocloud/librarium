The page lists security notices detailing CVEs found in OS images and Kubernetes binaries used to provision workload
clusters across the various deployment models. Only critical and high vulnerabilities are listed based on National
Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) severity.

:::info

The CVSS Severity is provided by either a third-party service provider, or NIST CVE. We do not provide the criticality
score for third-party components.

:::

To fix all the vulnerabilities impacting your products, we recommend patching your instances to the latest version
regarding any third-party components. For vulnerabilities originating in our products, we will provide mitigations and
workarounds where applicable.

By default, the table is sorted to display descending entries that were recently modified. Click on the CVE ID to view
the full details of the vulnerability.
